AiSleep Smart Pillow

Management & Technology

AiSleep Website

The AiSleep Mat is a smart pillow insert for sleep deprived students who are unaware of the importance of managing their sleep cycle. It provides an automated solution to their sleep schedule and can be inserted into any king-size pillowcase to track sleep. The Mat automatically records data in the AiSleep software and tracks four metrics: duration of sleep, movement throughout the night, external temperature, and noise level.

Used Arduino Uno, implemented a series of pressure/force sensors and motion trackers in order to measure the various sleep stages that the user goes through during the night. These sensors will collect data that will be uploaded to our webapp, and charts will be created to give the user a visualized method of garnering their sleep information. Furthermore, the data that is being collected will be used to give the user feedback on how to improve their sleep for future nights.

  • Date: July 2018
  • Category: Startup Company
